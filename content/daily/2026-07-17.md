### [Singer Jesy Nelson calls plan to test newborns for deadly muscle condition a 'victory'](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c07y77exx8eo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss)

- 英國宣布將對所有新生兒進行脊髓性肌肉萎縮症（SMA）的篩檢，此為一項由牛津大學主導的大型研究計畫。SMA是一種嚴重的遺傳性疾病，會導致肌肉無力，嚴重時甚至致命。此舉之所以重要，是因為新興的基因療法能夠有效治療SMA，但必須在症狀出現、造成不可逆的損害前進行，因此全國性的新生兒篩檢將能及早發現病例，讓嬰兒及時接受治療，從而顯著改善其預後。
- England will begin screening all newborn babies for spinal muscular atrophy (SMA) as part of a major study led by the University of Oxford. SMA is a severe genetic disease causing progressive muscle weakness that can be fatal. This initiative is significant because new gene therapies are highly effective but must be administered before symptoms cause irreversible damage, making early diagnosis through universal screening critical for transforming patient outcomes.
