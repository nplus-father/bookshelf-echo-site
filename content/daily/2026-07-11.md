### [UniClawBench: A Universal Benchmark for Proactive Agents on Real-World Tasks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08768v1)

- 研究人員推出了 UniClawBench，一個用於評估主動式 AI 代理在真實世界任務中表現的新基準測試。與現有的沙盒環境不同，UniClawBench 在即時 Docker 容器中評估代理，涵蓋 400 項任務，旨在測試長文本推理和跨平台協調等五種核心能力。其重要性在於，它提供了一種更真實、更精細的代理性能評估方法，幫助開發者區分基礎模型的內在能力與代理框架的設計，從而更準確地診斷並改進代理系統。
- Researchers introduced UniClawBench, a new benchmark for evaluating proactive AI agents on real-world tasks. Unlike existing sandboxed benchmarks, UniClawBench uses live Docker containers and a novel closed-loop feedback system to assess agents across 400 tasks targeting five core capabilities, such as long-context reasoning and cross-platform coordination. This matters because it provides a more realistic and fine-grained evaluation environment, helping developers disentangle a base model's inherent abilities from the agent framework's design to better diagnose and improve agent systems.

### [Ideas Have Genomes: Benchmarking Scientific Lineage Reasoning and Lineage-Grounded Idea Generation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08758v1)

- 研究人員推出了 IdeaGene-Bench (IG-Bench)，一個用於評估 AI 系統理解科學思想演化能力的新基準測試。該基準將科學進程比擬為生物演化，把論文表示為「思想基因組」，並追蹤其繼承與變異。目前的語言模型在此基準上表現不佳，最強模型準確率僅 27.3%，這揭示了模型在理解複雜科學知識譜系方面存在顯著瓶頸，而此能力是 AI 成為真正科研夥伴的關鍵。
- Researchers introduced IdeaGene-Bench (IG-Bench), a new benchmark that tests an AI's ability to reason about the evolution of scientific ideas. The framework treats papers as 'Idea Genomes' and tracks their lineage through concepts like inheritance and mutation. Current LLMs perform poorly, with the top model achieving only 27.3% accuracy on reasoning tasks, revealing a significant 'compositional bottleneck' in their ability to understand and build upon complex scientific knowledge.

### [Score Accuracy Along the Forward Diffusion Does Not Certify Numerical Stability in Diffusion Sampling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08757v1)

- 一篇研究論文證明，擴散模型的分數函數即使在前向過程中非常準確，也不能保證逆向採樣過程的數值穩定性。研究人員構建了理論和實驗案例，顯示即使模型訓練誤差極小，其離散化採樣器（如 Euler-Maruyama）仍可能產生罕見但極端的異常值，導致生成分佈的矩發散。此發現揭示了標準擴散採樣器的一個潛在失效模式，並提出對於具有已知邊界支持的數據，可透過將去噪器輸出投影到該邊界內來恢復穩定性。
- A research paper demonstrates that high accuracy in a diffusion model's score function during the forward (noising) process does not guarantee numerical stability during reverse-time sampling. The authors construct theoretical examples where a model with arbitrarily small training error produces a sampler (e.g., Euler-Maruyama) whose moments diverge due to rare but extreme outlier trajectories. This highlights a potential failure mode in standard diffusion samplers and suggests a fix for data with known bounded support: projecting the denoiser's output onto that known set restores stability.

### [vsov/okfy — Knowledge engineering for AI agents: distill corpora too big for any context window into small, verified, git-native knowledge bundles](https://github.com/vsov/okfy)

- okfy 是一個新的知識工程工具，能將大型語料庫（如程式碼庫或文件）提煉成小型的、經過驗證的、git原生知識包，以解決知識庫過大無法放入AI代理上下文視窗的問題。其核心特點是採用確定性方法，使用BM25（而非向量）進行可重複、可審計的檢索，並強制要求開發者親自驗證知識包的品質。這種工程優先的設計，為建構可靠的RAG系統提供了一個比傳統向量檢索更可控、更值得信賴的替代方案。
- okfy is a new tool for distilling large corpora like codebases into small, verified, git-native knowledge bundles for AI agents, addressing the problem of context windows being too small for entire knowledge bases. Its core is deterministic, using BM25 search instead of vectors for repeatable, auditable retrieval. Critically, it enforces a human-in-the-loop workflow where an owner must verify the bundle's quality before release, offering a more reliable and controllable alternative to standard vector-based RAG systems.
